How it works

Each letter is shifted forward (encrypt) or backward (decrypt) by the chosen value in the alphabet.

🔢

Shift range

Values 1–25 are valid. A shift of 13 is the well-known ROT13 variant, its own inverse.

🔡

Case preserved

Uppercase and lowercase letters are shifted independently. Numbers and symbols are left unchanged.
